## ⚠️ What to Avoid

### Link Building DON'Ts
- ❌ Buying links from PBNs
- ❌ Link farms or networks
- ❌ Excessive exact-match anchors
- ❌ Irrelevant/spammy sites
- ❌ Comment spam
- ❌ Auto-generated content
- ❌ Hidden links

### Outreach DON'Ts
- ❌ Generic mass emails
- ❌ Pushy follow-ups
- ❌ Fake personalization
- ❌ Lying about relationships
- ❌ Promising things you can't deliver
